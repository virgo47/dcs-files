DCS World

  • Corrected flight dynamics of AIM-120 missiles based on our latest CFD research. All changes increased the launch range by about 10-20%.
    AIM-120C got next changes:
    - Significantly reduced subsonic and transonic zero-lift drag.
    - Slightly increased of siupersonic zero-lift drag.
    - Reduced lift-induced drag.
    - Increased lift.
    AIM-120B got next changes:
    - Reduced lift-induced drag.
    - Increased lift.
    Both missiles received an updated calculation of thrust altitude dependency. Because of this, the thrust at altitude has become somewhat less, and the maximum speed is less.
  • AI F-16C. Corrected landing gear compression on the ground.


DCS F-16C Viper by ED

  • 3D cockpit model. Eliminated some gaps and stitches in the forward part of the cockpit.
  • Increased length of afterburner effect, slightly decreased diameter.
  • Corrected afterburner textures. WIP.
  • Flashlight can be used without aircraft battery power.
  • 3D model. Corrected wing flex animation with lights and pylons.
  • Ground refueling fixed when battery is off
  • AIM-9 Uncage Needed for Radar Slave - Fixed
  • Sidewinder Seeker Diamond won't move to target - Fixed
  • When the pylons are set to be removed in the Mission Editor, they missile rails still appear - Fixed

DCS A-10C Warthog by ED

  • 3D Cockpit. Fixed glowing sticker "Zeroize".
  • 3D Cockpit. Fixed reflections on the instrument’s glass.
  • 3D Cockpit. Fixed texture bug "UNF -> UHF"
  • 3D Cockpit. Restored missing 801 and 804 arguments.
  • 3D Cockpit. Corrected tint of side glasses of windshield.
  • 3D Cockpit. Fixed bad "Fire <APU> Handle" textures.

DCS F/A-18C Hornet by ED

  • Restored missing TWS L&S Data
  • Corrected the heading vector of target tracks.

DCS AJS-37 Viggen by Heatblur Simulations

  • Fixed RAT clipping through intake
  • Fixed missing RAT textures
  • Fixed missing Pilot Oxygen hose
  • Fixed last sidewinder causing CTD

DCS Normandy 1944 by UGRA-Media

  • Trees: LOD switching improvement, palette added
  • Models: Notredame de Rouen - texture corrected, textures of bridges improved, LOD switching for water tower also improved
  • Airfields:
    • Season texture glitches removed from new airfields
    • Taxiways repositioned on Picauville, Needs Oar Point, Funtington, Chailey
    • Glidepath for Cretteville, Saint-Pierre cleared from obstacles
    • Wind cones added for Biniville, Longues-sur-Mer
    • Wind cone removed from Barville taxiway
  • Typo for “Cheux” name fixed
  • England Landscape is improved (partially)
  • Corrected some road objects and structures
  • Flooded pontoon bridge raised above river level
  • Fixed erroneous autumn texture in Spring pack
